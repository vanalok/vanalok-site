Our Founders

Santosh Sutar

Founder

  • Highly energetic and a multifaceted personality with diverse interests — the brains behind the inception of Vanalok.
  • 25 years of expertise in Environmental Education, creating interpretation centres, publishing educational and environmental content, and creating forest trails across Karnataka, Maharashtra and Goa.
  • Expert in teacher training programs and student-led project based learning.
  • Awardee of many local and state level awards for work in Environmental Education and implementation of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • Environmental Consultant to different educational institutions, government institutions and departments.

Vishnupriya Hathwar

Founder

  • Highly energetic and a multifaceted personality with diverse interests. Has worked with different sectors pertaining to Education and Nature.
  • 20 years of expertise in Environmental Education and Nature-based Integrated Learning.
  • Author of "The Invisible Link" — a book on integrated learning, and science-toonist.
  • Mentor to students for project based learning and personal development.
  • Conducts Nature based Educator Training Programs and credit courses for students.
